# Dinner at La Grande Boucherie

## Restaurant Details
**Name:** La Grande Boucherie  
**Address:** 17 W 38th St, New York, NY 10018
**Phone:** 212-382-1600

## Overview  
La Grande Boucherie is a contemporary French brasserie located in Midtown Manhattan, celebrated for its vibrant atmosphere and exquisite cuisine. With a menu featuring a blend of classic French dishes and modern interpretations, it aims to offer a memorable dining experience for both locals and visitors. 

## Menu Highlights  
- **Steak Frites:** A classic French dish, featuring a perfectly cooked steak accompanied by golden fries.  
- **Escargots:** Tender snails prepared with garlic butter and herbs, a true French delicacy.  
- **Duck Confit:** Succulent duck leg slow-cooked to perfection, served with crispy potatoes.  
- **Crème Brûlée:** A luscious dessert with a caramelized sugar top, offering a delightful contrast in texture.  
- **Wagyu Carpaccio:** Thinly sliced premium Wagyu beef, drizzled with olive oil and topped with capers.  
- **Niçoise Salad:** A fresh blend of tuna, green beans, hard-boiled eggs, and olives, dressed in a vinaigrette.  

## Summary of Reviews  
La Grande Boucherie has received an impressive rating of **4.8/5** from over **3,000 reviews**. Diners rave about the restaurant's lively ambiance and top-notch service, alongside its delicious food. Many reviews highlight the **Steak Frites** as one of the best in the city, while the **Escargots** are celebrated for their rich flavor and perfect preparation. Customers appreciate the attentiveness of the staff and the restaurant's ability to cater to both casual meals and special occasions.

Whether you're a seasoned gourmet or trying French cuisine for the first time, La Grande Boucherie promises a delightful culinary journey.
